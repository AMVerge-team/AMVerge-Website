# Introduction

AMVerge turns scrubbing through long episodes into seconds. It splits any video
into individual scenes and lays them out as a grid, so you find the exact shot
fast, no timeline scrubbing required.

<Media src="/clips/AMVerge-Video.mp4" caption="AMVerge in action: an episode split into a browsable scene grid." />

## What it does

Instead of dragging across a timeline, AMVerge renders an entire episode as a
clip grid. Scan, click, export. What might take 30 minutes in a traditional
editor drops to under 5.

- **Browse visually** — every scene as a tile, not a thin timeline.
- **Zero quality loss** — clips export as a 1:1 copy of the source.
- **Stay organized** — folders, saved imports, and merge to clean up cuts.

## Who it's for

AMVerge is built for AMV editors and anyone who needs to pull clips out of video
quickly: finding a specific scene, skimming an episode, or building a montage.
