# HEVC (H.265) Support

In the top right of the app there's a **HEVC status indicator**. It tells you
whether your PC can decode H.265 / HEVC video, which affects how fast previews
load for many newer encodes.

## Why it matters

- **HEVC supported** : previews for HEVC encodes load much faster.
- **HEVC missing** : some videos need conversion before they can preview, which
  slows things down.

## Recommendation

Installing the official **Microsoft HEVC Video Extension** is worth it for the
smoothest experience with modern encodes.

The top bar also shows whether the *currently loaded* video is HEVC, so you can
tell at a glance when conversion might be involved.
