# Episode Panel

The Episode Panel on the left sidebar is where imported episodes and folders
live. It lets you organize past imports and revisit them quickly.

## Organizing

- **New folder** — create a folder (header button) to group related episodes.
- **Drag & drop** — move episodes in and out of folders to organize them.
- **Sort** — toggle the sort order from the header.

## Rename & delete

Right-click an episode or folder for its context menu:

- **Rename** — change the display name.
- **Delete** — remove the episode/folder from the panel. With multiple episodes
  selected, you can delete them all at once.

## Clear Episode Panel

The **Clear** action removes every episode from the panel and deletes their
cached files on disk to free space.

> This only removes AMVerge's cached clip data. It never deletes your original
> episode or movie files.

Clearing the whole panel is also available in [General Settings](/docs/settings-general).

## Storage

Imported data is saved so reopening the app is fast. Change where it lives with
**Episodes Storage Path** in [General Settings](/docs/settings-general).
