# Appearance

Open **Settings** from the sidebar, then the **Appearance** tab to customize how
AMVerge looks.

## Theme

| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Accent color** | The primary color used for buttons, highlights, and icons. |
| **Background Gradient** | Secondary color for the background gradient effect. |

## Background media

| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Background media** | Upload a custom image, GIF, or video as the app background. |
| **Background opacity** | Transparency of the background media. |
| **Background blur** | Blur amount applied to the background media. |

## Clips & grid

| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Grid preview speed** | How fast clip previews play in the grid. |
| **Show download button** | Toggle the per-clip download button. |
| **Show clip timestamps** | Toggle timestamp labels on clips. |
| **Widescreen clip tiles** | Switch tiles between square (1080x1080) and widescreen (1920x1080). |

## Reset

**Factory Reset** here reverts all appearance and theme settings to their
defaults (it does not clear your episodes).
