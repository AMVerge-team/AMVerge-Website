# Importing Episodes

Load a video into AMVerge and let it split into scenes automatically.

## Supported input

Any anime episode, movie, or video file. AMVerge feeds it through scene
detection and builds a clip grid.

## Steps

1. Click **Import Episode** in the top bar.
2. Pick a video file.
3. Wait for detection. Scenes appear in the grid as they finish.

> Tip: keep related episodes in one folder, then use the Episode Panel to
> jump between them without re-importing.

## What happens under the hood

Detection finds scene cuts (I-frame based), then each scene is cut into its
own clip and a preview thumbnail is generated.
