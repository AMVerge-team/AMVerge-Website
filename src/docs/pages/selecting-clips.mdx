# Browsing & Selecting

Scan the grid, focus a clip into the preview, and pick the scenes you want.

## Focusing a clip

**Single click** a clip to focus it and load it into the preview panel on the
right. Focusing does not select, it just previews.

## Selecting multiple clips

| Action | Result |
|---|---|
| **Single Click** | Focus a clip into the preview (no selection) |
| **Ctrl + Click** (Cmd on macOS) | Add or remove individual clips from the selection |
| **Double Click** | Quick-add a clip to the selection |
| **Shift + Click** | Select every clip between the focused clip and the one you click |

## Selected count

The toolbar shows **N selected**. Tick its checkbox off to clear the whole
selection at once.

## Tips

- Don't overthink selection early. Grab anything that looks useful while
  skimming, you can refine later.
- If a fight scene or fast sequence got split into several clips, select them
  together and [merge](/docs/merging-scenes) them into one.
