# Merging Scenes

Combine related cuts back into one seamless segment.

## Why merge

No scene detector is perfect. Fast motion, flashes, camera shakes, or fight
sequences can create extra cuts. Instead of rebuilding clips in an editor,
merge them back together inside AMVerge.

## How to merge

1. Select the clips you want joined (see [Selecting Clips](/docs/selecting-clips)).
2. Check **Merge Clips**.
3. Export. Selected clips become a single output file.

Leave **Merge Clips** unchecked to export each clip separately instead.

> Merging also keeps your project panel clean: fewer, larger scenes are faster
> to browse and import into your editor.
